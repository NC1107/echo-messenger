## Quality Scoring

Sources are scored on a 1-10 scale across 5 dimensions:
- **Authority** (3x weight): Official docs, recognized experts, established sites
- **Recency** (2x weight): Within 2 years for tech topics
- **Depth** (2x weight): Comprehensive coverage, not superficial
- **Examples** (2x weight): Includes code/practical demonstrations
- **Uniqueness** (1x weight): Offers different perspective

Maximum composite score: 100
